How to Apply for PAN Card Online & Offline

A Permanent Account Number (PAN) is a ten-character alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department of India under the supervision of the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT). It serves as a universal identification key for tracking all financial transactions of an individual or entity.

PAN is mandatory for filing income tax returns, opening bank accounts, making high-value transactions (above ₹50,000), buying/selling property, purchasing vehicles, and many other financial activities. Every Indian citizen, NRI, or entity engaged in financial activities in India should possess a PAN card.

Important to Know
  • PAN application fee is ₹107 (+ GST) for Indian communication address and ₹1,017 (+ GST) for foreign address
  • You can apply through NSDL (Protean) or UTIITSL portals
  • PAN card is typically delivered within 15-20 working days after successful application
  • You can track your PAN application status using the Acknowledgment Number
  • An e-PAN (digital PAN) is sent to your registered email within 48 hours of allotment
  • Aadhaar-based Instant e-PAN is available FREE of cost on the Income Tax portal

Documents Required for PAN Application

Note: You need to provide documents for Proof of Identity (POI), Proof of Address (POA), and Proof of Date of Birth (POB). One document from each category is sufficient. Some documents serve multiple purposes.
Proof of Identity
  • Aadhaar Card (issued by UIDAI)
  • Voter ID / Election Commission Photo ID Card
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • Ration Card with Photograph
  • Arm's License
  • Photo ID issued by Central/State Government
  • Pensioner Card with Photograph
  • Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) Card
  • Bank Certificate with attested photograph
Proof of Address
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • Post Office Passbook with address
  • Latest Bank Account Statement/Passbook
  • Electricity Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Landline/Broadband Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Water Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Property Tax Assessment Order
  • Domicile Certificate from Government
  • Rent Agreement with Notary
Proof of Date of Birth
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Authority
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • Matriculation Certificate (Class 10th)
  • Mark Sheet from recognized board
  • Pension Payment Order
  • Marriage Certificate issued by Registrar
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Affidavit sworn before a Magistrate
For Minor (below 18 years)
  • Birth Certificate of the minor
  • School Certificate
  • PAN of Parent/Guardian (any one parent)
  • Identity proof of Parent/Guardian
  • Documents showing relationship with the minor

Note: Photograph and signature of parent/guardian is required on the form. No photograph of minor below 5 years is needed.

Method 1: Apply for PAN Online via NSDL (Protean) Portal

The NSDL (now rebranded as Protean eGov Technologies) portal is the most popular way to apply for a new PAN card online. You can complete the entire process from home.

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Visit the NSDL PAN Portal
    Go to onlineservices.nsdl.com — the official NSDL PAN application page.
  2. Select Application Type
    Choose "New PAN - Indian Citizen (Form 49A)" from the dropdown.
    For foreign citizens, select "Form 49AA".
    Select the Category: Individual, HUF, Company, Firm, Trust, etc.
  3. Fill Personal Information
    Enter the following details accurately:
    • Full Name (First, Middle, Last — as per documents)
    • Date of Birth
    • Email Address (e-PAN will be sent here)
    • Mobile Number
    • Enter the Captcha code displayed
    Click "Submit" to proceed.
  4. Receive Token Number
    After submitting, you will receive a Token Number on your registered email.
    Click on the link in the email or use the token number to continue your application.
  5. Complete the PAN Application Form
    Fill in all the remaining sections of the form:
    • Personal Details: Father's/Mother's Name, Gender, Aadhaar Number
    • Source of Income: Salary, Business, Capital Gains, etc.
    • Communication Address: Full address with PIN code
    • Document Details: Select proof documents for Identity, Address, and Date of Birth
  6. Choose Document Submission Mode
    Select one of the following options:
    • Submit digitally through e-KYC & e-Sign (Aadhaar-based): No physical documents needed — fastest method
    • Submit scanned images through e-Sign: Upload scanned copies of documents online
    • Forward application documents physically: Post the documents to the NSDL office
  7. Upload Documents (if applicable)
    Upload scanned copies of your proof documents:
    • Photo (JPEG, max 50KB)
    • Signature (JPEG, max 50KB)
    • Proof of Identity document
    • Proof of Address document
    • Proof of Date of Birth document
  8. Make Payment
    Pay the application fee of ₹107 (+ GST) using:
    • Credit/Debit Card
    • Net Banking
    • UPI
    • Demand Draft
  9. e-Sign / Aadhaar Authentication
    If you chose digital submission, authenticate using Aadhaar OTP.
    An OTP will be sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile number. Enter the OTP to digitally sign your application.
  10. Download Acknowledgment
    After successful submission, download and save your Acknowledgment Receipt.
    Note down the 15-digit Acknowledgment Number — you will need this to track your application.
If physical submission was chosen: Print the Acknowledgment, affix 2 passport-sized photographs, sign it, and send it along with supporting documents to: Income Tax PAN Services Unit, Protean eGov Technologies Limited, 5th Floor, Mantri Sterling, Plot No. 341, Survey No. 997/8, Model Colony, Near Deep Bunglow Chowk, Pune – 411016 within 15 days.

Method 2: Get Instant e-PAN (FREE — Aadhaar Based)

The Income Tax Department offers a completely FREE Instant e-PAN service for individuals who have a valid Aadhaar with a mobile number linked to it. This is the fastest way to get a PAN — issued within 10 minutes!

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Visit Income Tax e-Filing Portal
    Go to incometax.gov.in — Instant e-PAN
  2. Click on "Get New e-PAN"
    On the Instant e-PAN page, click the "Get New e-PAN" button to start the process.
  3. Enter Aadhaar Number
    Type your 12-digit Aadhaar number in the field provided.
    Read and accept the terms & conditions, then click "Continue".
  4. OTP Verification
    An OTP will be sent to the mobile number linked to your Aadhaar.
    Enter the 6-digit OTP and click "Continue".
  5. Validate Aadhaar Details
    Your details (Name, Date of Birth, Gender) will be auto-fetched from Aadhaar.
    Verify the details and click "Confirm".
  6. e-PAN Allotted!
    Your PAN is generated instantly.
    You can download your e-PAN (PDF) immediately from the same page.
    The e-PAN is also sent to your Aadhaar-linked email address.
Eligibility: This method is available only for individual applicants who have a valid Aadhaar with a mobile number registered with UIDAI. You must NOT already have a PAN allotted to your Aadhaar.

Method 3: Apply for PAN Online via UTIITSL Portal

UTIITSL (UTI Infrastructure Technology and Services Limited) is the second authorized agency for PAN card processing in India.

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Visit the UTIITSL Portal
    Go to pan.utiitsl.com
  2. Click on "Apply for New PAN Card (Form 49A)"
    Select the appropriate application type for Indian citizens.
  3. Fill in the Application Form
    Complete all required fields including:
    • Full Name, Date of Birth, Gender
    • Father's Name / Mother's Name
    • Aadhaar Number
    • Communication Address with PIN code
    • Contact Number and Email
    • Source of Income
  4. Upload Documents
    Upload scanned copies of required documents (Photo, Signature, Identity Proof, Address Proof, DOB Proof).
  5. Make Payment
    Pay the fee of ₹107 (+ GST) through Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.
  6. Submit and Track
    After payment, note down your Coupon Number for tracking the application.

Method 4: Apply for PAN Offline (Visit PAN Center)

You can apply for PAN offline by visiting a TIN Facilitation Centre (TIN-FC) or PAN Centre managed by NSDL or UTIITSL. This is ideal for those who prefer in-person assistance or are not comfortable with online processes.

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Locate the Nearest PAN Centre / TIN-FC
    Find a nearby centre using:
    • NSDL TIN-FC Locator
    • UTIITSL PAN Centre Locator
    • You can also find centres at select post offices and bank branches.
  2. Obtain Form 49A
    Collect Form 49A (for Indian citizens) from the centre.
    You can also download and print it from the Income Tax Department website.
  3. Fill the Form Carefully
    Use BLOCK/CAPITAL LETTERS only. Fill in:
    • Full Name (as per supporting documents)
    • Father's Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Gender
    • Complete Address with PIN code
    • Contact Number and Email
    • Aadhaar Number
    • Source of Income
    • Representative Assessee details (if applicable)
  4. Affix Photographs & Sign
    Paste 2 recent passport-sized photographs (one on the form, one on the acknowledgment).
    Sign or put thumb impression across the photograph on the form.
  5. Attach Supporting Documents
    Attach self-attested photocopies of:
    • Proof of Identity (1 document)
    • Proof of Address (1 document)
    • Proof of Date of Birth (1 document)
    Keep original documents with you for verification.
  6. Submit Form at the Centre
    Submit the filled form along with documents at the PAN centre counter.
    The executive will verify your originals and accept the application.
  7. Pay the Fee
    Pay ₹107 (+ GST) in cash, by cheque, or demand draft at the centre.
  8. Collect Acknowledgment Receipt
    You'll receive an Acknowledgment Receipt with a 15-digit acknowledgment number.
    KEEP THIS RECEIPT SAFE! You'll need the acknowledgment number to track your PAN status.
Processing Time: Your PAN card will be dispatched to your communication address within 15-20 working days from the date of receipt of the application.
