Common Issues

Errors in name spelling, date of birth, gender, or address on your Aadhaar card can cause issues with government services, banking, and more.

How to fix:

  • Online: Visit the UIDAI Self-Service Update Portal to update your name, date of birth, gender, or address using OTP verification. You may need to upload supporting documents (e.g., passport, birth certificate).
  • Offline: Visit your nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra with the original supporting documents and fill out the Aadhaar Update/Correction Form.

Note: Name change is allowed only once through the online portal. For further changes, visit an Aadhaar centre in person.

Fingerprint or iris scan failures during authentication are common, especially for elderly individuals, manual labourers, or those with worn fingerprints. This can prevent successful Aadhaar-based verification at banks, PDS shops, and other services.

How to fix:

  • Update Biometrics: Visit the nearest Aadhaar Enrolment Centre and request a biometric update. UIDAI recommends updating biometrics every 10 years.
  • Use Face Authentication: UIDAI has introduced Face Authentication as an alternative when fingerprint or iris authentication fails.
  • File a Grievance: If authentication keeps failing, lodge a complaint at the UIDAI Grievance Portal or call the toll-free helpline 1947.

After enrolment, Aadhaar generation typically takes 60–90 days, and the physical PVC card may take additional time for delivery via India Post.

How to fix:

  • Check Status Online: Track your Aadhaar generation status using your 14-digit Enrolment ID (EID) at the UIDAI Status Check Portal.
  • Download e-Aadhaar: Once generated, instantly download your e-Aadhaar (digitally signed PDF, equally valid as physical card) from the e-Aadhaar Download Portal.
  • Order PVC Card: If the physical card is lost or not received, order an Aadhaar PVC card (₹50 fee) at UIDAI PVC Order Page.
  • If it has been more than 90 days with no update, visit the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra or call 1947.

Aadhaar enrolment or update requests may be rejected due to insufficient or invalid documentation, incomplete application forms, poor-quality biometric captures, or duplicate Aadhaar detection.

How to fix:

  • Check Rejection Reason: Use your EID to check the status and rejection reason at UIDAI Status Portal.
  • Re-apply with Valid Documents: Ensure you carry original documents from the UIDAI Valid Documents List (e.g., Voter ID, Passport, PAN Card, Ration Card, Driving License).
  • Visit Aadhaar Centre: Re-enrol at the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra with correct documents.

Tip: Ensure your photograph and biometrics are captured clearly during the enrolment to avoid rejection.

Linking (seeding) Aadhaar with your bank account, PAN card, or SIM card may fail if there are data mismatches — for example, your name or date of birth differs between Aadhaar and the other service.

How to fix:

  • Aadhaar-PAN Linking: Link or check status at the Income Tax e-Filing Portal. If names don't match, first correct them in Aadhaar or PAN before linking.
  • Aadhaar-Bank Account Linking: Visit your bank branch with your original Aadhaar card and request seeding. Ensure the name and date of birth match your bank records.
  • Aadhaar-SIM Linking: Visit your mobile operator's store with your Aadhaar card for re-verification. You can also check if your mobile is linked via the TAFCOP Portal.
  • Fix Mismatches First: Update your Aadhaar details at the UIDAI Update Portal to ensure consistency across all linked services.

If you have lost or misplaced your physical Aadhaar card, you can easily retrieve your Aadhaar number and download a digital copy.

How to fix:

  • Retrieve Aadhaar Number: If you have forgotten your Aadhaar number, retrieve it using your registered mobile number at UIDAI Retrieve Lost UID/EID.
  • Download e-Aadhaar: Download your digitally signed e-Aadhaar PDF (equally valid as physical card) from the e-Aadhaar Portal using your Aadhaar number or EID and OTP.
  • Order PVC Reprint: Order a new Aadhaar PVC card online (₹50 fee) at UIDAI PVC Order Page.
  • Lock Your Aadhaar: If you suspect misuse, immediately lock your Aadhaar biometrics at UIDAI Lock/Unlock Portal or via the mAadhaar app.

If the enrolment operator missed linking your mobile number or entered it incorrectly, you will not receive the OTP required to download e-Aadhaar, use online self-service portals, or perform Aadhaar authentication.

How to fix:

  • Update Mobile Number If no mobile number is linked (no OTP possible), you must visit the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra with your Aadhaar card and a valid ID proof to add or change your mobile number.
  • Verify Linked Mobile: Check which mobile number is currently linked by calling the UIDAI helpline at 1947.

Important: A registered mobile number is essential for all online Aadhaar services. Always keep it updated.
