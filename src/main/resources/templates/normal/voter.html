How to Apply for Voter ID Card Online & Offline

The Voter ID Card, officially known as the Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC), is an identity document issued by the Election Commission of India (ECI) to all eligible Indian citizens who have registered as voters. It serves as the primary proof of identity at polling stations during elections.

Every Indian citizen who has attained the age of 18 years on or before the qualifying date (1st January of the year of revision of the electoral roll) is eligible to register as a voter. The Voter ID card contains a unique EPIC Number (10-character alphanumeric code), the voter's photograph, name, father's/husband's name, date of birth, gender, and address. It is also widely accepted as a valid identity proof for various government and private purposes.

Important to Know
  • Voter registration is completely FREE of cost — no fee is charged at any stage
  • You must be 18 years or older (as on 1st January of the current year) to register
  • You can apply through the Voter Helpline Portal, Voter Helpline App, or by visiting the ERO (Electoral Registration Officer) office
  • The election form for new voter registration is Form 6
  • You can download your e-EPIC (digital Voter ID) from the Voter Helpline portal
  • After registration, a Booth Level Officer (BLO) may visit your residence for physical verification
  • Processing typically takes 15-30 days after submission

Documents Required for Voter ID Registration

Note: You need to provide Proof of Age and Proof of Address (Ordinary Residence). The address proof must match the address you enter in Form 6. Aadhaar number linking is now mandatory as per ECI guidelines.
Proof of Age (any one)
  • Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Authority / Hospital
  • Class 10th / 8th Marksheet or Certificate
  • Indian Passport
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card / Aadhaar Letter
  • Driving License
  • Birth Certificate from Church (for Christians)
  • Affidavit from a First Class Magistrate (if no other document available)
Proof of Address / Ordinary Residence (any one)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Indian Passport
  • Driving License
  • Bank / Kisan / Post Office Passbook
  • Ration Card
  • Latest Electricity / Water / Gas / Telephone Bill
  • Income Tax Assessment Order
  • Latest Rent Agreement (registered)
  • Property Tax Receipt or Municipal Tax Receipt
  • Government-issued ID Card with Address
Proof of Identity (optional, for verification)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Indian Passport
  • Driving License
  • Government Photo ID Card
  • Bank Passbook with Photo

Note: Identity proof is used for BLO verification. The primary documents required are Age and Address proof.

Special Cases
  • Overseas Voters (NRIs): Use Form 6A with a valid Indian Passport
  • Shifting Constituency: Use Form 6 with proof of new address; your entry in the old constituency will be deleted
  • First-time voters (age 17+): Can pre-register; entry will be activated on turning 18
  • Homeless persons: Can register at the address of the nearest homeless shelter or the area where they ordinarily reside

Note: For NRI registration, submit Form 6A with your Indian Passport at the ERO of your original constituency.

Method 1: Apply for Voter ID Online via Voter Helpline Portal

The Voter Helpline Portal (formerly NVSP — National Voters' Service Portal) is the official online platform by the Election Commission of India. You can complete the entire registration process from home.

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Visit the Voter Helpline Portal
    Go to voterportal.eci.gov.in — the official Election Commission voter registration portal.
  2. Register / Login to Your Account
    If you are a new user:
    • Click on "Register" or "Sign Up"
    • Enter your Mobile Number
    • Verify using OTP sent to your phone
    • Create a Password for your account
    If already registered, simply Login with your mobile number and password.
  3. Click on "Register as New Voter"
    After logging in, navigate to "New Voter Registration" or click on "Form 6 — Application for New Voter Registration".
  4. Fill in Personal Details (Form 6)
    Complete the online Form 6 with the following information:
    • Full Name (in English and your regional language)
    • Father's / Mother's / Husband's Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Gender
    • Mobile Number & Email ID
    • Aadhaar Number (mandatory as per recent ECI guidelines)
  5. Enter Address Details
    Fill in your current residential address accurately:
    • State and District (from dropdown)
    • Assembly Constituency (auto-populated based on address)
    • House No., Street, Area, Locality
    • Town/Village, Post Office
    • PIN Code
    Note: Your constituency will determine which polling station you are assigned to.
  6. Upload Required Documents
    Upload scanned copies / photos of the following:
    • Recent Passport-sized Photograph (JPEG/PNG, max 200KB)
    • Proof of Age — Birth certificate, 10th marksheet, passport, etc. (PDF/JPEG, max 2MB)
    • Proof of Address — Aadhaar, Passport, Utility bill, Bank Passbook, etc. (PDF/JPEG, max 2MB)
  7. Declaration & Preview
    Read the declaration statement carefully and tick the checkbox to confirm:
    "I hereby declare that all the information given above is true to the best of my knowledge and belief."
    Preview all the details entered before final submission.
  8. Submit the Application
    Click "Submit" to submit your Form 6 application.
    You will receive a Reference ID / Application ID on the screen and via SMS.
  9. BLO Verification
    After submission, a Booth Level Officer (BLO) may visit your residence to verify:
    • Your identity and address
    • That you actually reside at the given address
    • Your original documents (for verification)
    Tip: Keep your original documents ready when the BLO visits.
  10. Receive Voter ID Card
    Once your application is approved by the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO), your name will be added to the electoral roll.
    You can:
    • Download your e-EPIC (digital Voter ID) from the portal
    • The physical EPIC card will be dispatched/distributed through the ERO office or BLO
Note: Save your Reference ID safely. You can use it to track your application status on the Voter Helpline portal.

Method 2: Apply via Voter Helpline Mobile App

The Voter Helpline App is the official mobile application by the Election Commission of India, available on both Android and iOS. The process is identical to the web portal but optimised for mobile use.

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Download the Voter Helpline App
    Google Play Store (Android)
    Apple App Store (iOS)
  2. Register & Login
    Open the app → Register with your Mobile Number → Verify via OTP → Set a password → Login.
  3. Select "New Voter Registration" (Form 6)
    From the dashboard, tap on "Apply for New Voter Registration" or "Form 6".
  4. Fill Personal & Address Details
    Enter your name, father's/mother's/husband's name, date of birth, gender, Aadhaar number, and complete residential address (same fields as the web portal).
  5. Capture / Upload Documents
    You can directly take photos of your documents using the phone camera or upload existing files:
    • Passport-sized photograph (can use selfie camera)
    • Proof of Age document
    • Proof of Address document
  6. Review, Declare & Submit
    Review all details, accept the declaration, and tap "Submit".
    You'll receive a Reference ID via SMS and in-app notification.
  7. Track Application
    Track your application status directly in the app under "Track Application Status".
Tip: The Voter Helpline app also lets you find your polling station, check the electoral roll, file complaints, and get election-related notifications.

Method 3: Apply for Voter ID Offline (Visit ERO / BLO Office)

You can apply for a Voter ID offline by visiting your local Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) office, Tehsildar/Taluk office, or the District Collector/Magistrate office. You can also submit your form to the Booth Level Officer (BLO) assigned to your area.

Step-by-Step Process:
  1. Obtain Form 6
    Collect Form 6 from your nearest ERO office, Tehsildar office, or any voter registration camp.
    You can also download and print it from:
    • Election Commission of India — Forms
    • Voter Helpline Portal — Download Forms
  2. Fill the Form Carefully
    Use BLOCK / CAPITAL LETTERS. Fill in all sections of Form 6:
    • Full Name (in English and regional language)
    • Father's / Mother's / Husband's Name
    • Date of Birth & Age
    • Gender
    • Complete Residential Address
    • Aadhaar Number
    • Mobile Number & Email
    • Part/Serial Number of existing entry (if shifting from another constituency)
  3. Affix Passport-Sized Photograph
    Paste 2 recent passport-sized colour photographs on the form at the designated places.
    Ensure the photograph is clear, with a white/light background.
  4. Attach Supporting Documents
    Attach self-attested photocopies of:
    • Proof of Age (1 document)
    • Proof of Address (1 document — must match the address on Form 6)
    Carry original documents for verification.
  5. Submit Form at the ERO Office
    Visit the office of the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) in your constituency.
    Submit the filled Form 6 along with all documents at the counter.
    The official will verify your documents and accept the form.
  6. Collect Acknowledgment Receipt
    You will receive an Acknowledgment Receipt with a Reference Number.
    KEEP THIS RECEIPT SAFE! Use this reference number to track your application status.
  7. BLO Verification Visit
    A Booth Level Officer (BLO) will visit your residence to verify:
    • That you reside at the address given in the form
    • Your identity by checking original documents
    • That you meet the eligibility criteria
    Cooperate fully with the BLO and keep documents ready.
  8. Receive Your Voter ID Card
    After the ERO approves your application:
    • Your name is added to the Electoral Roll
    • The EPIC (Voter ID card) is printed and distributed through the BLO or ERO office
    • You can also download the e-EPIC from the Voter Helpline portal
Processing Time: The entire process (from submission to card delivery) usually takes 15-30 days, depending on BLO verification schedules and the electoral revision cycle.

After Application — What Next?

Track Application Status

Visit Voter Helpline Portal
Login → Go to "Track Application Status" → Enter your Reference ID to check the current status of your application.

Download e-EPIC (Digital Voter ID)

Visit Voter Helpline Portal
Login → Click on "Download e-EPIC" → Verify via OTP → Download your digital Voter ID card in PDF format.

Search Your Name in Electoral Roll

Visit Electoral Search
Search by name or EPIC number to verify your registration on the electoral roll.

Find Your Polling Station

Visit Electoral Search
After your registration is approved, you can find your assigned polling booth details by searching with your EPIC number.
