How to apply for Aadhar Online & Offline!

Step 1: Gather Required Documents

        
Before applying for a birth certificate, ensure you have the following documents ready:

        
  • Proof of birth (hospital discharge summary or notification).
    • 
            
  • Parent's identification documents (e.g., Aadhar card, passport, etc.).
    • 
            
  • Proof of address.
    • 
            
  • Marriage certificate of parents (if required).

          
Step 2: Choose Application Method

        
You can apply for a birth certificate through:

        
  • The local municipal office.
    • 
            
  • Online via the official government website.

        
Step 3: Complete the Application

        
Fill in the application form with accurate details, including:

        
  • Name of the child.
    • 
            
  • Date, time, and place of birth.
    • 
            
  • Parent's full names and other required information.

        
Step 4: Submit the Application

        
Submit the application along with the required documents either:

        
  • In person at the municipal office.
    • 
            
  • Online via the government portal.

         
Step 5: Track and Collect Your Birth Certificate

        
After submission, you can track the status of your application. Once approved, collect your birth certificate:

        
  • Physically from the municipal office.
    • 
            
  • Download it online (if available).
